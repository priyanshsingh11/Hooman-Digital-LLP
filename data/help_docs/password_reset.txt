Resetting Your Lumen Password

If you cannot access your Lumen account, you can reset your password using the standard recovery workflow.

To reset your password:
1. Navigate to the Lumen login page.
2. Select "Forgot Password".
3. Enter the email address associated with your account.
4. Check your inbox for a password reset link.
5. Open the link and create a new password.

Password reset links expire after 60 minutes for security reasons. If your link has expired, repeat the process to generate a new one.

If you do not receive the reset email:
- Check spam or junk folders
- Verify that your email address was entered correctly
- Allow up to 10 minutes for email delivery
- Ensure your mail provider is not blocking noreply@lumen.example

Enterprise environments using SSO (Single Sign-On) may not support direct password resets through Lumen. In these cases, users must contact their internal IT administrator.

Accounts protected with Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA) may require backup recovery codes during login. If MFA codes are unavailable and recovery codes were lost, customers must contact Support for manual verification.

For security purposes, Lumen Support cannot disclose passwords or bypass identity verification procedures.

Recommended password requirements:
- Minimum 12 characters
- At least one uppercase letter
- At least one lowercase letter
- One number and one symbol
- Avoid reused passwords from other services

Repeated failed login attempts may temporarily lock the account for 15 minutes. Excessive login failures originating from suspicious IP addresses may trigger additional security reviews.

If your account appears compromised:
- Reset your password immediately
- Revoke active sessions from Security Settings
- Enable MFA
- Contact Security Support if unfamiliar login activity continues
