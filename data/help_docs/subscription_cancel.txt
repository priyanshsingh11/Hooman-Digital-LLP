Cancelling a Lumen Subscription

Customers may cancel their Lumen subscription at any time through Workspace Settings > Billing > Manage Subscription.

Cancellation stops future automatic renewals but does not immediately delete workspace data unless specifically requested.

Monthly subscriptions remain active until the current billing period ends. Annual subscriptions continue through the prepaid contract term unless otherwise stated in a signed agreement.

To cancel a subscription:
1. Open Workspace Settings
2. Navigate to Billing
3. Select Manage Subscription
4. Click Cancel Plan
5. Confirm cancellation reason

Workspace owners are the only users authorized to cancel subscriptions.

After cancellation:
- Premium features become inaccessible at the end of the billing cycle
- Workspaces may be downgraded to Free status
- Certain advanced integrations are disabled
- Data retention policies still apply

Enterprise contracts may require notice periods before cancellation becomes effective. Customers under annual enterprise agreements should contact their Customer Success Manager for contract-specific terms.

Deleting a workspace is separate from cancelling a subscription. Customers who want permanent removal of account data must submit a deletion request through Support.

Common cancellation issues:
- Subscription already renewed before cancellation was submitted
- Cancellation performed under the wrong workspace
- Billing owner permissions missing
- Browser extensions interfering with checkout or billing pages

If customers experience problems cancelling online, Support may manually assist after verifying account ownership.

Refund eligibility after cancellation depends on account usage, subscription type, and billing timing. Please review the Lumen Refund Policy before submitting refund requests.

Accounts with unpaid invoices may remain in past_due status even after cancellation until outstanding balances are resolved.
